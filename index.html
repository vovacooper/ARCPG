The app is a framework for designing AR games and applications, using Qualcomm's Vuforia. It is based on the idea that a picture is the gateway to a common augmented reality world. Hence, if two (or more) people look at the same picture, they will all collaborate in the same AR space. As a proof of concept we provide two games: Snooker and Tanks. Note that there is no AI, the assumption is that the players play against each other. Think of it as your travel size collection of board games, tucked away in your cell-phone.
